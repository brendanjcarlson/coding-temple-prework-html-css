Hello, my name is Brendan Carlson, and I am excited to give you a glimpse into myself here on this page. I want to give you insight on where I came from and why Web Development is where I am going. I am currently based in Hurst, Texas near Fort Worth but am originally from Abilene, Texas. I am passionate about designing clean and interesting projects. I look forward to building my skills in these next few weeks and am excited to continue learning.

Before starting on this path, I was pursuing a career in the medical field. In undergraduate school, I studied Psychology and Pre Medicine at Abilene Christian University. After studing for and taking the MCAT while working as an Emergency Medical Technician, I realized I wanted to pursue a different career path. I hope to be a part of a team and contribute to useful and accessible technologies.

When I am not chipping away at myself led studies in web development, I am putting my energy towards animal rescue projects in my community or traveling to help communities around the world. I reflect on my three trips to Costa Rica where my family, friends, and community members put in plenty of elbow grease, even during the height of the pandemic. Those trips solidified my desire to not only produce quality products but to build them with all people in mind.
